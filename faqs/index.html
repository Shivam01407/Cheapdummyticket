What are dummy tickets airlines?

The term "dummy ticket" is another synonym for "flight reservation," which refers to booking  flight seats, fares, or both. Low-Cost-Carrier (LCC) and regular airlines are the two main categories of airlines. Making a dummy ticket is not possible when flying with a low-cost carrier, but it is doable when flying with another airline. Dummy ticket airlines are hence another term for LCC airlines. Air India, Qatar Airways, Lufthansa, Air Canada, British Airways, Air France, Emirates, Etihad, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines, United Airlines, Singapore Airlines, and others are a few of them. Indigo, GoAir, Air Asia, Air Arabia, and other LCCs are a few examples.

A dummy ticket on any airline other than LCC will be provided to you. It doesn't matter whatever airline's dummy ticket you use to apply for your visa as long as it has a valid PNR.
